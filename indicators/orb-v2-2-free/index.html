// ORB v2.2 — FREE EDITION

systematic opening-range strategies for futures.

ORB v2.2 Free is the open core of the ORBAlpha engine. it captures the opening range of your chosen futures session, waits for a confirmed breakout, manages the trade with configurable stop loss and take profit logic, and exits cleanly at session close.

asset-agnostic. works on any futures contract you trade, full-size or micro, on any intraday timeframe.

// WHAT IT DOES
  • captures the opening range of your chosen session
  • waits for a confirmed breakout above or below the range
  • manages the trade with configurable stop loss and take profit
  • exits cleanly at session close
  • sizes positions from your target risk and max risk per trade
  • filters by day-of-week
  • displays live stats: P/L, drawdown, win rate, profit factor
